Johnny Deep

John Christopher Depp II (born June 9, 1963) is an American actor and musician. He is the recipient of multiple accolades, including a Golden Globe Award and a Screen Actors Guild Award, and has been nominated for three Academy Awards and two BAFTA awards.

Depp made his feature film debut in the horror film A Nightmare on Elm Street (1984) and appeared in Platoon (1986), before rising to prominence as a teen idol on the television series 21 Jump Street (1987–1990).

In 2012, Depp was one of the world's biggest film stars> and was listed by the Guinness World Records as the world's highest-paid actor>, with earnings of US$75 million> in a year.
